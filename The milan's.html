Our Family Story

The Beginning

Segun and Kemi met in their hometown and fell in love. They got married in a beautiful ceremony surrounded by family and friends, marking the start of the Omilana family journey.

Habeeb Arrives

The family grew with the birth of their first child, Habeeb. As the firstborn, he brought immense joy and marked the beginning of their parenting journey.

Mariam Joins

Two years later, Mariam was born, completing the sibling duo. Her arrival brought a new dynamic to the family as the first daughter.

Harafat's Arrival

The family welcomed their third child, Harafat, who brought extra energy and laughter into the household with her vibrant personality.

Al-ameen Completes the Family

The youngest, Al-ameen, was born, completing the Omilana family. As the baby of the family, he receives love and attention from all his older siblings.

The Milan's Today

Today, the Omilana family (affectionately called "The Milan's") continues to grow stronger together. Each member contributes their unique personality to create a loving, supportive, and fun family environment.
